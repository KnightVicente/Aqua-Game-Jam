About the Jam

The goal of the jam is to make the most unique, stylized, and fun game within the theme and submission deadline.

Rules

  • ✔ You can work alone or in a team.
  • ✔ Use assets that you have the legal right to use.
  • ✔ You can use any engine you prefer.
