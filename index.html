About the Jam

The Freshwater Game Jam is an exciting challenge where participants create games based on the beauty and mysteries of freshwater environments. Whether you're an indie developer, student, or hobbyist, this jam is a chance to push your creativity, experiment with new mechanics, and collaborate with fellow game enthusiasts.

Speakers & Judges

  • Alex Rivers – Indie Game Developer, creator of AquaQuest
  • Dr. Lily Wade – Marine Biologist & Gamification Expert
  • Jordan Pike – Pixel Artist & Animator

Venue & Schedule

Venue: Online (Submissions via itch.io, communication via Discord)

  • Day 1: Theme reveal, team formation, and kickoff
  • Day 2: Development phase, mentoring sessions
  • Day 3: Submission deadline, showcase, and judging
